# Bloat details

### From tob resource folder disc (by karma/krish)
#### Bloat Walk
- The first down takes a minimum of 39 ticks and a maximum of 51 ticks
- If bloat toggles between walking and running or turns he will not be able to go down during the next 5t
- Turns have a 32t cooldown
- There is a 1/4 chance that bloat goes down on every tick that he can fall on
- There is a 1/16 chance that bloat will turn on every tick that it is off cooldown
- Bloat will always fall on tick 47 unless he has turned within the last 5t
- Once bloat drops below 60% HP his run will toggle on
- Once bloat drops below 40% HP his walk will toggle on and every subsequent attack will toggle his run back and forth

### Dimension details
- The room is 16x16 tiles
- Bloat is 5x5 tiles
    - Means 11 ticks to reach the edge if walking
    - Means 6 ticks to reach the edge if running
